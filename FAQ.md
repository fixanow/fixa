# Frequently Asked Questions

**Last Updated: May 12, 2026**

---

## General

### What is Fixa?

Fixa is a marketplace platform that connects homeowners with verified service professionals (Pros). Whether you need a quick repair, a full renovation, or regular maintenance, Fixa lets you describe your issue, receive competitive bids from local Pros, and get the job done — all from your phone.

### Is Fixa free to use?

Fixa is free to download and create an account. Customers pay only for the services they book through the platform. Pros pay a service fee on completed jobs. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges.

### Where is Fixa available?

Fixa is currently available in Spain. We are expanding to more regions — stay tuned for updates.

### What languages does Fixa support?

The app is available in English, Spanish (Español), Catalan (Català), and Turkish (Türkçe). You can change the language anytime in your app settings.

### How do I contact Fixa support?

Email us at **support@fixanow.com** or use the in-app Help & Support form in Settings. We typically respond within 24 hours.

## For Customers

### Getting Started

#### How do I create a Customer account?

1. Download Fixa from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Open the app and sign up with your email address and phone number.
3. Verify your phone number via SMS.
4. Complete your profile with your name and address.

#### What information do I need to provide?

- Your full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Service address (you can add multiple addresses)

We do not ask for payment information upfront — you only provide it when accepting a bid.

---

### Booking a Service

#### How do I request a service?

1. Open the app and describe your issue — type a description and add photos.
2. Confirm your service address.
3. Submit your request.
4. Pros in your area will review your request and send bids.
5. Compare bids, check Pro ratings and reviews, and accept the one that works for you.

#### What kind of services can I request?

Fixa covers a wide range of home services, including:

- Plumbing
- Electrical work
- Painting and decorating
- Carpentry and furniture assembly
- Appliance repair and installation
- Locksmith services
- General handyman tasks
- Air conditioning and heating
- Tiling and flooring
- Cleaning and maintenance

If you're unsure whether your job fits, submit it anyway — Pros will let you know if they can help.

#### Can I upload photos of my issue?

Yes. Adding photos helps Pros understand the scope of work and provide more accurate bids. You can take photos directly in the app or upload from your gallery. Up to 5 photos can be attached per request.

#### How long does it take to receive bids?

Most requests receive their first bid within minutes. The number and speed of bids depend on your location, the type of service, and current Pro availability. You'll receive a push notification each time a new bid arrives.

#### How many bids will I receive?

There is no set limit. Multiple Pros may bid on your request, and you can choose any of them — or none at all.

#### How do I choose the right Pro?

When evaluating bids, consider:

- **Price** — Compare the offered amounts.
- **Rating** — Check the Pro's overall star rating and read written reviews.
- **Response time** — How quickly did they bid?
- **Profile** — View the Pro's verified badges, qualifications, and portfolio.
- **Message** — Use in-app chat to ask questions before accepting.

#### Can I negotiate the price with a Pro?

The bid price is the Pro's initial offer. You can use in-app chat to discuss the scope of work and negotiate before accepting a bid. Once you accept, the price is agreed upon.

#### What happens after I accept a bid?

- The Pro is notified and confirms the booking.
- You'll receive the Pro's contact information and estimated arrival time.
- You can communicate through in-app chat or video call to coordinate details.
- The Pro arrives at the scheduled time, completes the work, and marks the job as done.
- You review the completed work and submit payment.

### Payments

#### How do I pay for a service?

Payments are processed securely in the app via Stripe. You can pay with:

- Credit or debit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Apple Pay or Google Pay (where available)

Payment is only charged after the service is completed.

#### Is my payment information secure?

Yes. Fixa does not store your raw card details. All payment data is handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant — the highest security standard in the payments industry.

#### When am I charged?

You are charged only after the Pro marks the job as completed and you confirm the work is done. No upfront charges.

#### Will I receive an invoice?

Yes. A receipt is automatically generated and available in the app under your booking details after payment is processed.

#### What if the final price differs from the bid?

If the Pro determines that additional work is needed beyond the original scope, they should communicate this through in-app chat before proceeding. Any price changes must be mutually agreed upon. If you did not agree to a higher price, contact support at **support@fixanow.com**.

---

### Cancellations

#### Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. You can cancel a booking at any time before the Pro begins work. Cancellation fees may apply depending on timing:

| Timing | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| More than 24 hours before service | None |
| 12–24 hours before service | 25% of estimated price |
| Less than 12 hours before service | 50% of estimated price |
| After Pro has arrived | Full price may apply |

#### How do I cancel?

Go to your booking details in the app and tap "Cancel Booking." The cancellation fee (if any) will be displayed before you confirm.

### Reviews

#### How do I leave a review?

After a service is completed, you'll be prompted to rate your experience. You can rate:

- **Overall rating** (1–5 stars)
- **Category ratings**: Punctuality, Quality, Communication, Cleanliness
- **Written comment** (optional, up to 500 characters)

#### Can I edit or delete my review?

Reviews cannot be edited after submission. If you believe your review contains an error, contact **support@fixanow.com**.

#### What if I had a bad experience?

We're sorry to hear that. Please:

1. Leave an honest review so other customers are informed.
2. Report the issue through the app or email **support@fixanow.com**.
3. Our team will investigate and take appropriate action.

## For Professionals

### Getting Started

#### How do I become a Pro on Fixa?

1. Download Fixa from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Sign up and select "Join as a Professional."
3. Complete your profile with your specializations, experience, and service area.
4. Upload verification documents (ID, licenses, insurance).
5. Our team reviews your application — typically within 48 hours.
6. Once approved, you start receiving job requests in your area.

#### What documents do I need to provide?

- Government-issued photo ID
- Professional licenses or certifications (relevant to your trade)
- Proof of liability insurance (where applicable)
- Proof of address

Requirements vary by trade and region.

#### Is there a vetting process?

Yes. All Pros go through:

- Identity verification
- License and certification checks
- Insurance verification
- Background screening (where permitted by law)

Only verified professionals can bid on customer requests.

---

### Bidding and Jobs

#### How do I receive job requests?

Once approved and your profile is active, you'll receive push notifications for new job requests in your service area. You can also browse available requests in the app.

#### How do I submit a bid?

1. Review the customer's request — description, photos, and location.
2. Assess the scope of work and estimated time.
3. Submit your bid with your proposed price and availability.
4. Wait for the customer to accept or decline.

#### Can I update or withdraw my bid?

Yes. You can modify or withdraw your bid at any time before the customer accepts it. Once accepted, the bid becomes a binding agreement.

#### How many jobs can I bid on?

There is no limit. You can bid on as many jobs as you want, as long as you can commit to the ones that get accepted.

#### What happens when a customer accepts my bid?

- You receive a notification with the booking confirmation.
- The customer's address and contact details become available.
- You can communicate via in-app chat or video call.
- Arrive at the scheduled time, complete the work, and mark the job as done.
- Payment is processed and disbursed to your account.

#### How do I mark a job as completed?

In the booking details screen, tap "Mark as Completed." The customer will be notified and prompted to review and confirm payment.

### Payments and Earnings

#### How and when do I get paid?

Payments are processed via Stripe and deposited to your registered bank account. Payouts are typically issued within **3–5 business days** after the customer confirms payment.

#### What fees does Fixa charge?

Fixa charges a service fee on each completed job. The current fee rate is displayed in the app under your Pro profile settings. This fee covers:

- Platform maintenance and development
- Payment processing
- Customer support
- Marketing to bring you more customers

There are no upfront or subscription fees.

#### Can I see my earnings history?

Yes. Go to your Pro profile in the app and tap "Earnings" to view:

- Total earnings
- Per-job breakdown
- Pending payouts
- Fee deductions

#### What if a customer doesn't pay?

Fixa collects payment from customers at the time of booking confirmation or job completion. If a payment issue arises, our support team handles it — you don't need to chase payments.

### Profile and Reputation

#### How do I improve my profile?

- **Complete all fields**: Add a professional photo, bio, specializations, and portfolio.
- **Verify your credentials**: Upload licenses and certifications for verified badges.
- **Maintain high ratings**: Deliver quality work and communicate well.
- **Respond quickly**: Fast response times improve your visibility.

#### How do ratings work?

After each job, customers rate you on:

- **Overall experience** (1–5 stars)
- **Punctuality**, **Quality**, **Communication**, **Cleanliness**

Your average rating is displayed on your profile and influences your ranking in search results. Pros with higher ratings receive more job requests.

#### Can I respond to a negative review?

Currently, reviews are one-directional (customer to Pro). If you believe a review is unfair or inaccurate, contact **support@fixanow.com** and our team will investigate.

#### What can cause my account to be suspended?

- Repeated cancellations of accepted bookings
- Consistently poor ratings (below 3.0 average)
- Fraudulent behavior or misrepresentation
- Violating our Terms and Conditions
- Failing to show up for scheduled jobs
- Accepting payments outside the platform

## Troubleshooting

### I'm not receiving push notifications.

1. Check that notifications are enabled in your device Settings > Fixa.
2. Make sure "Do Not Disturb" or "Focus Mode" is not active.
3. Try logging out and back into the app.
4. If the issue persists, contact **support@fixanow.com**.

### The app is crashing or not loading.

1. Make sure you're using the latest version — check the App Store or Google Play for updates.
2. Force close the app and reopen it.
3. Restart your device.
4. Check your internet connection.
5. If the problem continues, contact **support@fixanow.com** with your device model and OS version.

### I can't upload photos.

- Ensure Fixa has camera and photo gallery permissions in your device settings.
- Photos must be under 10 MB each.
- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, HEIC.
- Maximum 5 photos per booking request.

### My payment failed.

- Check that your card is valid and has sufficient funds.
- Ensure your billing address matches the one on file with your bank.
- Try a different payment method.
- If the issue persists, contact your bank or **support@fixanow.com**.
