## How It Works

1. **Describe your issue** — Take a photo, write a description, and share your location.
2. **Get bids from Pros** — Verified professionals in your area send competitive offers.
3. **Choose your Pro** — Compare ratings, reviews, and prices. Pick the right fit.
4. **Get it done** — Your Pro arrives, completes the work, and you pay securely in the app.

## For Customers

- Browse verified and rated service professionals
