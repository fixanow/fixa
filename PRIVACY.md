- Responding to lawful requests from public authorities.

**Legal basis:** Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR).

## 8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

### 8.1 Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten") (Art. 17 GDPR)

You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations. We will delete data that is no longer necessary for its original purpose or for legal compliance.

### 8.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18 GDPR)

You may request that we restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances, such as when you contest the accuracy of the data.

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

You may request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (e.g., JSON), and you have the right to transmit that data to another controller.

### 8.6 Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)

You may object to the processing of your personal data when it is based on legitimate interest. We will cease processing unless we have compelling legitimate grounds.

### 8.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Art. 22 GDPR)

You have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling, that produce legal effects or similarly significant effects. We do not currently use automated decision-making for such purposes.

### 8.8 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **support@fixanow.com** with:

- Your registered email address.
- A description of the right you wish to exercise.
- Any relevant details to help us locate your data.

We will respond to your request within **30 days** as required by GDPR. In complex cases, this period may be extended by an additional 60 days, and we will inform you of the extension and reasons.

## 10. Location Data

The App requests access to your device's location services for the following purposes:

- **Matching**: Connecting Customers with Pros in their service area.
- **Estimates**: Providing accurate estimated arrival times and service availability.
- **Navigation**: Helping Pros navigate to the Customer's service location.

### 10.2 Your Choices

## 13. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

- **Encryption**: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+. Sensitive data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.
- **Authentication**: JWT-based authentication with token refresh mechanisms.
- **Access control**: Role-based access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.
- **Infrastructure security**: Helmet security headers, rate limiting, input validation, and regular security updates.
- **Payment security**: All payment data is processed through Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant.
- **Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring for unauthorized access, anomalies, and security incidents.

Despite our best efforts, no system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach:

- We will notify the relevant supervisory authority within **72 hours** of becoming aware, as required by GDPR Art. 33.
- We will notify affected users without undue delay when the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms (GDPR Art. 34).
